About Us

We are the World's largest University

Nuthana University of Science and Technology(NUST) is the world's largest university based on student enrollment. As of 2024, it has over 7.14 million active students. This vast enrollment is achieved through its extensive network of distance and online education programs, offering courses at certificate, diploma, and degree levels. IGNOU operates in India and has study centers in over 15 countries, making it accessible to a global student body.
